Client Project

POWER GROUNDNUT SHELLER

Developed a complete SOLIDWORKS CAD model and assembly of a Power Groundnut Sheller based on client-provided engineering specifications for an academic project. My role was limited to 3D CAD modeling and assembly creation. The mechanical design and research were provided by the client.

Project Overview

The Power Groundnut Sheller is an agricultural machine developed to separate groundnut kernels from their shells efficiently. This SOLIDWORKS model was created based on client-provided engineering specifications for an academic research project.

My Contribution

  • Created accurate 3D CAD models of individual components.
  • Built the complete assembly in SOLIDWORKS.
  • Maintained dimensional accuracy based on client specifications.
  • Prepared the model for engineering visualization.

Note: My responsibility was limited to CAD modeling and assembly creation. The original machine design and research were provided by the client.
